Texas

Texas is a state located within the south central portion of the United States. It is bordered by Mexico to the south, Louisiana and Arkansas to the East, Oklahoma to the North and New Mexico to the west. The capitol of Texas is Austin, major cities include Houston, San Antonio, Dallas, Austin, Fort Worth and El Paso.

Texas has been part of 6 different nations; Spain, France, Mexico, Republic of Texas, United States of America and the Confederate States of America.

Natural high point: Guadalupe Peak, 8,750ft of elevation above sea level in the Guadalupe Mountains of West Texas.

Natural low point: The Gulf Coast of Mexico, 0ft elevation at sea level.

Texas is rich in diversity of landscape with the Hill Country in parts of Central and South Texas, prairies in North and Central Texas, Pine forests in East Texas, the Gulf Coast of Mexico along the southeast portion of the state, high plains and canyons in the Panhandle and mountains and deserts in West Texas.
