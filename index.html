Texas

Texas is a state located in the south-central United States bordering Mexico to the south, New Mexico to the west, Oklahoma to the north and Louisiana to the east.Throughout its history, Texas has been part of six different countries; Spain, France, Mexico, the Republic of Texas, the United States and the Confederate States.

Capitol: Austin

State Flower: Bluebonnet

State dish: Chili

Highest Natural Point: 8,750ft above sea level, Guadalupe Peak

Lowest Natural Point: 0ft sea level, Gulf Coast of Mexico

Major cities in Texas include (but not limited to): Houston, San Antonio, Dallas, Fort Worth, Austin, El Paso, Amarillo, Waco, Corpus Christi and Laredo

Professional sports teams: Dallas Cowboys (NFL), Houston Texans (NFL), Houston Astros (MLB), Texas Rangers (MLB), Dallas Mavericks (NBA), San Antonio Spurs (NBA), and Houston Astros.

Major Landmarks: Big Bend National Park, Guadalupe Mountains National Park, the Alamo, Hemisphere Tower, San Antonio Riverwalk, Texas State Capitol Building, University of Texas tower, AT&T Stadium, NASA Space Center, Fort Worth Stockyards, Stevie Ray Vaughan statue and more.
