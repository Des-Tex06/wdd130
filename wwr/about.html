History

Back in the summer of 1991, two college buds, Dan and Brad along with their friends went up to Bear Canyon for the weekend for some rafting. After experiencing the exhilerating rush of the rapids through the Grizzly river in Bear Canyon, these two college buds decided that they wanted to experience this weekend, again and again! During that weekend to never forget, the White Water Rafting Company was born!
