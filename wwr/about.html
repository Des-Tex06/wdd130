History

White Water Rafting started in the summer of 1999 when a group of friends, Greg, Rick, Steve and Ally went to the Rattlesnack Rapids. After an exhilerating experience rushing the rapids and thankfully no rattlesnake bites, the old crew decided that they wanted to continue their adventure by starting a new venture known as White Water Rafting Company!
