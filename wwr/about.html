History

In the summer of 1999, a group of friends; Greg, Rick, Steve and Ally went on a roud trip to the Rattlesnack Rapids. Rushing through the streams and sailing over the rapids the crew of friends found that they were having the adventure of a lifetime!

After an exhilerating experience rushing the rapids and thankfully no rattlesnake bites, the old crew decided that they wanted to continue their adventure by sharing it with the world by starting a new venture known as White Water Rafting Company!
