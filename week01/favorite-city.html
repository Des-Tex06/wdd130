Dublin, Ireland, My Favorite City

Dublin, or Baile Átha Cliath in the Irish Language is the largest city on the island of Ireland and the capital of the Republic of Ireland and is located on the east coast of Ireland in the Province of Leinster (Cúige Laighean in the Irish language) with the River Liffey flowing through the city into the Irish Sea or Muir Éireann.

Dublin contains a rich abundance of history with medieval, Georgian and Victorian architure taking the form of castles, cathedrals, pubs, libraries, colleges, and bridges. Dublin Castle (built in the 13th Century), Trinity College Dublin (founded in 1592), The Long Room (a great library located at Trinity College Dublin where the Book of Kells is reposited), the Custom House, the General Post Office, the Samuel Beckett Bridge, Temple Bar are just a few examples of the many historical sites in Dublin.
